⚠️Common scams here

▲
Fake taxi "broken meter"
If a driver says the meter's broken, agree a price first or use Bolt. Airport taxis should always run the meter.
▲
Tram 28 pickpockets
The crowded 28 is a hotspot. Keep bags in front, phones away from open windows at stops.
▲
"Free" restaurant couvert
Bread and olives brought unasked are charged. Wave them away if you don't want them.
▲
Euronet ATMs
The blue Euronet machines add heavy fees. Use bank (Multibanco) ATMs instead.
