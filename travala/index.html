Why onchain

Reviews you can trust, because no one can rig them.

Regular review sites can be gamed — businesses buy fake stars and delete the bad ones. Travala writes every rating to the blockchain, tied to a real traveler's wallet.

  • ✓ Each comes from a wallet with a real travel history
  • ✓ Rate manually, or switch on auto-sign to skip the taps
